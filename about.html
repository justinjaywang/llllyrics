about

What is llllyrics?

A collaboratively edited lyrics website.

What songs are on llllyrics?

Any song that has been added by the community. If you can't find a song you're looking for, feel free to add the lyrics yourself. Just no Taylor Swift songs, please.

Can I make an edit to a song?

Yes, if you see a mistake, please do. The link appears when you hover over the song title.

Can I delete a song?

No.

Does llllyrics support search operators?

Yes. You can use quotes to search for an exact phrase and prefix operators to restrict your search to the artist, album, or song title. For example:
"on a beech tree rudely carved" artist:belle_&_sebastian

Why make yet another lyrics site?

This one has been designed for speed and readability on all devices. There are no ads or pop-ups. Only lyrics. And for the power users, there are a number of keyboard shortcuts to be discovered.

Who made llllyrics?

A guy in the Bay Area who loves music and design.
