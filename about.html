About

What is llllyrics?

A collaboratively edited lyrics website.

What songs are on llllyrics?

Any song that has been added by the community. If you can't find a song you're looking for, feel free to add the lyrics yourself. Just no Taylor Swift songs, please.

Can I make an edit to a song?

Yes, if you see a mistake, please do. The link appears when you hover over the song title.

Can I delete a song?

No.

Does llllyrics support search operators?

Yes. You can use quotes to search for an exact phrase and prefix operators to restrict your search to the artist, album, or song title.

"on a beech tree rudely carved" artist:belle_&_sebastian

Why make yet another lyrics website?

This one has been designed for speed and readability, on all devices. No ads or pop-ups. Only lyrics. And for the experts, there are a number of keyboard shortcuts.

rreturn to previous search
hgo home
eedit a song you're viewing
vview a song you're editing
+add a new song
Escescape input focus
/set input focus
Entergo to first search result

Who made llllyrics?

Just a dude in San Francisco who loves music and design.
