about

What is llllyrics?

A collaboratively edited lyrics website.

What songs are on llllyrics?

Any song that has been added by the community. If you can't find a song you're looking for, feel free to add the lyrics yourself. Just no Taylor Swift songs, please.

Can I make an edit to a song?

Yes, if you see a mistake, please do.

Can I delete a song?

No.

Is there advanced search?

Yes. You can use quotes to search for an exact phrase or the prefix operators artist:, album:, and song: to restrict your search to an artist, album, and song title.

Why make yet another lyrics website?

This one has been designed for speed and readability. No ads or pop-ups. Only lyrics.

Who made llllyrics?

Just a dude in California who loves music and design. Feedback is welcome.
